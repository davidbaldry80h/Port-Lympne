A Christmas short break at Port Lympne offers an experience like no other, allowing you to relax and soak up the festive spirit with family, friends or partners while we take care of every little detail.

Our team understand that every Christmas is unique and will work with you to include some of your personal family traditions in your stay. If you need a hearty supply of fresh cookies to leave out for Santa or a sprig of mistletoe hung above your door, just us know and we would be delighted to help.

With 11 different ways to stay, we are proud to offer something for everyone, from a stay in our luxurious 4-star boutique hotel, to treehouses nestled high above the woodland canopy, romantic 180-degree panoramic bubble pods and lavish immersive lodges partially set within lions' and tigers' habitat.

Guests will enjoy the warmest of welcomes and an unforgettable safari staycation with all the luxurious touches you would expect from this once-in-a-lifetime experience.
